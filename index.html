Chapter 1
Obey the Laws
The Law of Cause and Effect
  • Success is predictable
  • Law of Belief: What you believe with feeling becomes reality
  • Law of Expectations: You get what you expect
  • Law of Attraction: You attract what you think about
  • Your outer world reflects your inner world
Chapter 2
Decide Exactly What You Want
Clarity is Power

  • Select your #1 goal
  • Take immediate action
  • Only 3% have written goals - they accomplish 5-10X more
  • Review daily and visualize as accomplished
  • Unwritten goals are just wishes
Chapter 3

  • Take action despite fear
  • Build on small wins
  • Step out of comfort zone regularly
  • Courage is not absence of fear
Chapter 4
Be True to Yourself

  • Integrity is most important for long-term success
  • Keep your word always
  • Live in alignment with values
  • Be the same person in public and private
  • Build trust and inner peace
Chapter 5
Positive Mental Attitude
Your Attitude Determines Altitude
  • You become what you think about most
  • Control your thinking continuously
  • Practice gratitude daily
  • Look for the good in every situation
  • Associate with positive people
  • 95% Rule: Say only what you want to be true
Chapter 6
Expect the Best
The Law of Expectations
  • What you expect with confidence becomes self-fulfilling prophecy
  • Expect best from yourself, others, and situations
  • Pygmalion Effect: People perform to your expectations
  • Use "YET" instead of "can't"
Chapter 7
